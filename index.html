I'm Kevin Moseni, a Ph.D candidate in Materials Sciences and Engineering at University of California, Riverside.

I use computational methods to study the effects of symmetry breaking on the properties of materials. The materials I've focused on have various applications, including sensing, ultrafast magnetic memory, and quantum information science.
